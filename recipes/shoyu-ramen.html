Shōyu Ramen・醤油ラーメン

Description

Ramen is a japanese doodle dish. It generally consists of chinese-style noodles that are served in a broth and accompanied by
various toppings (meat, seafood, vegetables). Different types of ramen exist, we'll be making Shōyu (soy sauce) ramen. Shōyu
ramen uses soy sauce and chicken based broth, and is accompanied by toppings like pork belly, egg, and green onions.

Ditch the cup ramen, try this stuff. It's bomb.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles, store bought, or made from scratch (link recipe)

  • Broth
    • chicken stock
    • soy sauce
    • garlic
    • ginger
    • green onion

  • Toppings
    • Chasu sliced pork belly
    • soft boiled egg
    • Naruto (pinwheel fish cake)
    • Menma (bamboo shoots)
    • Corn
    • Bean spouts
    • Sliced scallions

Steps

  1. Make the broth
  2. Cook the noodles, drain them, add them to another bowl
  3. Add noodles to the broth
  4. Add the toppings
