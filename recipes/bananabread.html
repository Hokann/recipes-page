Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is simple and easy to make. It's also crazy good. Because of that, it's become quite a famous type of bread to make.
On top of bananas, nuts, chocolate chips and fruit like blueberries can be added to the bread for added flavor and texture. It is also
possible to make Gluten-free banana bread, although this recipe will not cover it. See this Gluten-free banana bread recipe for more info.

This recipe provides around 10 servings from 1 loaf.
From start to finish, it should take around an hour to prepare the bread.
The ingredients are fairly easy to obtain and inexpensive, unless maybe if you shop at Whole Foods.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 bananas
  • 1/3 cup of (melted) butter
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Mash bananas in a mixing bowl, add butter, and stir
  3. Mix in the baking soda, salt, sugar, beaten egg, vanilla extract. Then add the flour (in that order)
  4. Pour the mix in a buttered loaf pan
  5. Bake for 55 to 65 minutes at 350°F (175°C)
  6. Let cool completely and serve. Enjoy!
